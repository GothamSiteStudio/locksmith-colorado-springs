Frequently Asked Questions

Common Locksmith Questions from Colorado Springs Customers

How fast can you get to me if I am locked out?

We prioritize lockout calls and aim to arrive as quickly as possible. Our mobile service covers all of Colorado Springs and surrounding areas, so response times are typically fast. Call us at (719) 257-3108 and we will give you an estimated arrival time.

Do you make car keys for all vehicle makes and models?

We are equipped with professional key-cutting machines and programmers that cover most vehicle makes and models. This includes standard car keys, transponder keys, key fobs, and push-to-start remotes. Call us with your vehicle details and we will let you know right away.

Should I rekey my locks after buying a new home?

Yes, rekeying is one of the most important security steps after purchasing a home. You never know how many copies of the old keys are out there. We can rekey all your locks quickly and affordably so only your new keys work.

Do you install electronic and smart locks?

Absolutely. We carry a variety of electronic code locks, smart deadbolts, and keyless entry systems. Whether you need an upgrade for your home or business, we can supply and install the right solution for your needs.

What commercial locksmith services do you offer?

We handle business lockouts, lock rekeying, master key systems, panic bar installation, high-security locks, electronic access control, and door hardware installation. We work with all types of commercial properties including storefronts, offices, and warehouses.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Locksmith Solutions LLC is a licensed and insured locksmith business operating in Colorado Springs and El Paso County. We are committed to honest, professional service on every job.
