Key Types We Program

Chip Keys, Fobs, Smart Keys, and Remotes

Different vehicles use different key technologies. Here are the main types we program on site:

  • Transponder chip keys - The most common type. A small chip embedded in the key head sends a unique code to the immobilizer. New chips must be programmed to your specific vehicle.
  • Key fobs with remote buttons - Combined key and remote units that lock, unlock, and sometimes start the vehicle. Both the physical key and the remote functions need to be synced.
  • Flip keys - Keys where the metal blade folds into the fob body. These typically contain both a transponder chip and remote buttons that require programming.
  • Smart proximity keys - Keyless entry and push-to-start systems where you carry the key in your pocket. These require pairing with the vehicle's receiver system.
  • Standalone remotes - Separate remote control units (no key blade) that operate locks and sometimes the trunk or panic alarm.

Frequently Asked Questions

Transponder Key Programming Questions

What is a transponder key and why does it need programming?

A transponder key has a small electronic chip embedded in the head of the key. When you insert the key and turn the ignition, the chip sends a signal to the car's immobilizer system. If the signal does not match, the engine will not start. A new or replacement transponder key must be programmed to match your specific vehicle before it will work.

Can you program a transponder key without going to the dealership?

Yes. We use professional automotive programming tools that allow us to program transponder keys, key fobs, and smart keys on site at your location. No dealership visit or vehicle towing needed.

How long does transponder key programming take?

Most transponder key programming is completed in 15 to 30 minutes on site. The exact time depends on the vehicle make, model, and the type of key being programmed.

Can you program a key fob or push-to-start remote?

Yes. In addition to traditional transponder chip keys, we program key fobs, flip key remotes, smart proximity keys, and push-to-start remotes for most vehicle brands.

What vehicles do you support for transponder programming?

We support most domestic and foreign vehicle brands including Toyota, Honda, Ford, Chevy, Nissan, Hyundai, Kia, Dodge, Jeep, Subaru, Volkswagen, BMW, Mercedes, and many more. Call us with your vehicle details and we will confirm coverage before dispatching.
