Common Situations

When to Call for Car Lockout Help

Car lockouts happen for many reasons. Here are the most common situations where our customers call for mobile vehicle entry service:

  • Keys locked inside the car - You stepped out and the doors locked with your keys on the seat or in the ignition.
  • Dead key fob battery - Your remote will not unlock the doors and you do not have a physical backup key.
  • Broken key in the lock - The key snapped inside the door lock or ignition cylinder and needs extraction.
  • Lost or stolen keys - You cannot find your keys and need immediate access to your vehicle.
  • Trunk lockout - Your keys are locked in the trunk and the release is not accessible from outside.
